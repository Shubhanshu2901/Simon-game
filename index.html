Press any Key to Start

Rules are as follows :

  • For every level the computer will add one color to the game sequence, i.e. the sequence keeps getting longer for every level.

  • The objective for the player is to enter the game sequence for as long as they can manage, entering the wrong sequence will end the game.

  • For eq: if at level 1 - green flashes, at level 2 - red flashes and at level 3 - blue flashes, the player must click green, red, blue to clear level 3.

  • The game will go on as long as player keeps answering correctly.
